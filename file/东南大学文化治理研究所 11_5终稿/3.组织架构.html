OUR TEAMS
东南大学文化治理研究所现有校内研究人员10名，校外人员4名，所有成员都在文化、哲学、戏剧、影视等方面卓有成绩。
客座团队：国际著名导演、国家著名编剧等各种牛逼的大名鼎鼎的人物

研究中心：来自东南大学、南京大学等的跨学科、高素质人才研究团队。




戏梦社区文化艺术传播中心是一家以繁荣城市戏剧教育艺术、关怀文创发展为宗旨的政府正式注册的民办非企业，综合运用微电影、线上APP研发、艺术创意工作坊等多种创新艺术方式，并结合社区特色及现有资源，创作出一批风格独具的在地戏剧,更使社区的艺术营造更具有人文气息。

青麦坊公益剧场：主体为扬州青麦文化传媒有限公司

Miss Shirley Lingyan Zou was graduated recently from Nanjing university in the year of 2015, School of Liberal Arts and was selected to join the Harvard SEED fellowship at the same time. Her degree is majored in Drama Literature, as an extension in order to realize her childhood interest in the ancient Greek mythology tales. As an exchange student in Tunghai University, Taiwan 2013, she had the privilege of working along side the celebrity artist Yaling Lang, who is famous for her "Stone" theoretical company in community art building. Inspired by Miss Lang's word that "artist should return to it's seedbed to develop and create", Miss Zou return to Nanjing, China and started the "DREAMA" Community Arts Centre.
